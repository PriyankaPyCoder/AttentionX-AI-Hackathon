Hackathon Project

AI Content Repurposing Engine

long-form video → viral short clips, auto-captioned & reframed to 9:16

01
STEP 1 🎙 Transcribe with Whisper transcriber.py

Runs OpenAI Whisper on the source video to produce word-level timestamps. Results are cached as JSON so you don't re-run Whisper on every iteration.

whisper.load_model() word_timestamps=True JSON cache 99 languages
02
STEP 2 🔊 Audio Energy Scoring audio_scorer.py

Librosa extracts RMS energy, zero-crossing rate, and spectral centroid for each segment. These three signals are combined into a normalized 0–1 audio score that detects laughter, raised voices, and applause.

librosa.load() RMS energy Spectral centroid ZCR 0–1 normalized
03
STEP 3 🪟 Merge into Clip Windows audio_scorer.py

Consecutive Whisper segments are merged into clip windows of 20–60 seconds. Each window gets an averaged audio score. This produces the candidate pool for ranking.

20–60s windows sliding merge min/max duration
04
STEP 4 🤖 LLM Sentiment + Keyword Scoring llm_scorer.py

GPT-4o-mini receives all windows in two batched calls — one for emotional sentiment scoring, one for keyword importance. Results are combined with audio scores using configurable weights into a single final_score.

gpt-4o-mini batched JSON sentiment 0–1 keyword 0–1 composite score
05
STEP 5 ✂️ Cut Clips with MoviePy clip_cutter.py

MoviePy's subclip() extracts the top N ranked windows from the source video with lossless audio passthrough. Clips are named with their rank and score for easy identification.

moviepy.subclip() libx264 codec AAC audio 30fps
06
STEP 6 📱 Reframe to 9:16 (MediaPipe) reframer.py

MediaPipe face detection samples every 15th frame to find the average face X position. A 9:16 crop window is centered on the face and the result is upscaled to 1080×1920 for TikTok/Reels.

mediapipe FaceDetection frame sampling 1080×1920 Lanczos upscale center fallback
07
STEP 7 💬 Captions + Hook Generation caption_burner.py · llm_scorer.py

PIL renders word-by-word karaoke captions — the currently-spoken word is highlighted in gold. GPT-4o-mini then generates one viral hook title (≤10 words) per clip to use as the video description.

PIL ImageDraw karaoke highlight CompositeVideoClip hook title GPT RGBA overlay
